About Me

I am currently a Research Scientist at the University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston, USA. My research focuses on bioinformatics, particularly in the analysis of WGS and RNA-Seq data, computational drug designing of fusion proteins, and the development of novel bioinformatics tools and resources.
